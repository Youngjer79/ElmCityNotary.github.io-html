ABOUT

If you need reliable notary services in a hurry, contact the insured professionals at Elm City Mobile Notary. Elm City Mobile Notary is a unique company specializing in general notarizations as well as a list of other services and best of all WE COME TO YOU! Elm City Mobile Notary is a reliable service servicing New Haven, Hamden,West Haven, East Haven, Branford,Milford, North Haven,Meriden, Waterbury, Wallingford,Orange, Southington and all surrounding areas. Our mobile notary signing agents are available to come to a home, office or just about anywhere else on short notice. We also travel to hospitals, rehabilitation facilities, nursing homes, coffee shops or anywhere notarizations are needed.

Why Choose Elm City Mobile Notary

Convenience:

We will meet you at a location that is most convenient for you at any time day or night.

Ease of Use:

Schedule our mobile notary services simply by filling out and submitting our online contact form. You can also give us a call 24 hours a day!

Insured Agents:

All our notary agents are covered by errors and omissions insurance policy for your protection and peace of mind.

Time Savings:

We’ll save you the time and hassle of finding a notary on short notice.

Repeat Scheduling:

Our services are perfect for professionals who routinely need to schedule signings, whether it’s on a daily, weekly or monthly basis.

No Extra Fees:

No Extra fees for last minute appointments, or out of ordinary hour appointments. (Additional fees are only for extended wait times beyond the scheduled appointment time or for additional services).
